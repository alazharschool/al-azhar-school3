We're a

It is an international, non-profit Egyptian Islamic school that strives to achieve solid pedagogical and educational goals. Our primary goal is to raise and educate Muslim children about the Islamic religion in an accurate and reliable manner. By studying the Holy Qur'an and the Sunnah of the Messenger of God, may God bless him and grant him peace. We are keen to facilitate the learning process by providing a stimulating learning environment that brings together the teacher and the student in an effective and productive way.

Services

Islamic Studies

Islamic Studies offers comprehensive education in Fiqh, Quranic interpretation, Hadith, Islamic history, and Usul al-Fiqh. It aims to provide flexible online courses with expert teachers, accredited certificates, and ongoing support for students of all levels, from beginners to advanced.

Book Now

Teaching Quran

Offers specialized Quran lessons, with a focus on correct recitation, Tajweed, and memorization. Includes interactive classes with experienced teachers, and provides a flexible online learning environment for students of all ages and levels.

Book Now

Arabic Language

It offers Arabic language lessons, from the basics of reading and writing to fluency. It focuses on building essential language skills such as grammar and vocabulary, and provides interactive and supportive lessons to meet the needs of learners of all levels.
